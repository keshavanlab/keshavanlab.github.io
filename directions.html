Directions to Massachusetts Mental Health Center:

The Massachusetts Mental Health Center is located in the Longwood Medical Area, at 75 Fenwood Road Boston, MA 02115. An image of laboratory map is available at the bottom of the page. For directions to our facility, and easy access through public transportation, see below:

Public Transportation-Green Line:
Take the Green Line, E train, get off at the Brigham Circle. Once you are off the T, walk down towards Brigham and Women's Hospital (Francis Street), at the corner of Francis Street and Binney Street turn left and walk down towards Fenwood Road. The Massachusetts Mental Health Center will be on your right.

Bus:
Take the 39 bus, which stops at Huntington Avenue and Francis Street. Once you have gotten at Huntington and Francis Street, walk down towards Brigham and Women's Hospital (Francis Street). At the corner of Francis Street and Binney Street turn left and walk down towards Fenwood Road. The Massachusetts Mental Health Center will be on your right.

Take the 66 bus, which stops at Tremont Street and Huntington Avenue. Walk down Francis Street towards Brigham and Women's Hospital. At the corner of Francis Street and Binney Street turn left and walk down towards Fenwood Road. The Massachusetts Mental Health Center will be on your right.

Both buses 60 and 65 stop at Brookline Avenue and Francis Street. Walk down Francis Street towards Brigham and Women's Hospital. At the corner of Francis Street and Binney Street turn right and walk down towards Fenwood Road. The Massachusetts Mental Health Center will be on your right.
