IMPACT Program

IMaging, PsychopAthology, Cognition and Therapeutics

The IMPACT Program is engaged in a variety of studies of the brain mechanisms underlying cognition and mood in healthy individuals and in those with psychiatric and medical disorders. The IMPACT Program also seeks to develop and evaluate new treatments for people with disorders of cognition and mood.

In the setting of our research programs and laboratories, our program provides a range of research training opportunities for psychiatric residents, post-doctoral fellows, medical students and graduate students. Our research and research training programs are supported through grants and endowments from the Federal Government, the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, Harvard Medical School, Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center, and research foundations, as well as through the generous contributions of individual donors. We hope that this website will provide a useful guide for interested students, research collaborators and clinical colleagues.
